Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

My Quant by GS ("the App") is a free iOS application that provides Monte Carlo stock simulations, AI-powered market sentiment analysis, and financial news. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes and how it is used.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Require you to create an account or log in
  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Track your location
  • use cookies or third-party analytics SDKs
  • Share any data with advertisers
  • Store any of your data on our servers beyond what is necessary to fulfill your request

2. Information the App Processes

When you use the App, the following data is processed to deliver the service:

  • Ticker symbols you search — the stock ticker you enter (e.g. "AAPL") is sent to our backend server solely to fetch price data, news, and run your simulation. It is not stored or associated with you.
  • Stock price data — retrieved from Yahoo Finance (yFinance) on demand. Prices are cached temporarily on our server to improve performance.
  • News articles — fetched from Alpha Vantage's News & Sentiment API based on the ticker you request. No personal data is transmitted to Alpha Vantage.
  • AI sentiment analysis — news headlines are sent to OpenAI's API to generate a market sentiment summary. No personal information is included in these requests. OpenAI's data usage is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

3. Local Storage

The App stores your last searched ticker symbol locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by deleting the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Yahoo Finance (yFinance) — stock price data
  • Alpha Vantage — financial news and sentiment scores
  • OpenAI — AI-powered sentiment analysis

These services have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by us. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 16.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
